Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about STAG.

General

STAG is a premium nightlife platform that connects people looking to enter exclusive clubs, bars, and lounges with verified hosts who can facilitate their entry. Males browse available hosts near their chosen venue and book a guaranteed, code-confirmed entry experience.
STAG is available to anyone who is at least 18 years old and of legal drinking age in their jurisdiction. You must provide accurate information when creating your account.
STAG is expanding to new cities regularly. Open the app and enable your location to see clubs and hosts available near you. If your city isn't live yet, sign up to be notified when it launches.

For Males

  • Browse nearby clubs based on your location.
  • Select a verified female companion whose profile you like.
  • Send a club entry request with the date and time.
  • Once she accepts, complete the in-app payment securely.
  • Meet her at the venue entrance and share the meetup code to confirm entry.
Yes. Your payment is held securely in escrow and is only released after both parties confirm the meetup. If the female companion cancels a confirmed booking, you receive a full refund automatically.
Cancellations made within the app's specified window (e.g., at least 2 hours before the booking) are eligible for a refund. Late cancellations are non-refundable. Check the app for exact cancellation terms at the time of booking.
Final entry to any venue is always at the discretion of that venue's management. STAG facilitates the connection and entry process, but cannot override a venue's own admission policies.

For Females (Hosts)

  • Create your profile and upload a photo.
  • Enable your location so nearby users can discover you.
  • Receive and review booking requests — you are never obligated to accept.
  • Accept only requests you are comfortable with.
  • Meet the user at the club, share the meetup code, and your earnings are transferred to your account.
No. Only your approximate area (neighbourhood / zone) is visible to other users. Your precise GPS location is never exposed in the app.
Once you and the user exchange the meetup code at the venue, the entry is confirmed and your earnings are transferred directly to the bank account linked to your profile. Add your bank details in the app's settings to receive payments.

Payments & Safety

STAG uses secure in-app payments. All major UPI apps, debit cards, and credit cards are accepted.
No. STAG does not store your payment details. All payment processing is handled securely in compliance with industry standards.
All users on STAG go through a phone-number verification process during sign-up. Additional identity checks may be required to maintain platform safety.

Account
