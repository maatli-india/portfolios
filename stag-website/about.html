About STAG

STAG is a premium nightlife platform that connects people for exclusive club nights and curated social experiences — on their own terms.

We believe a great night out should be effortless. No queues. No awkward waits. No uncertainty. STAG handles every step — discovery, connection, payment, and confirmation — so you can focus on what matters: enjoying the night.

For Him

For Him

Finding the right person to walk into a club with shouldn't be complicated. STAG lets you browse nearby clubs, discover verified female companions in the area, and send a club entry request in just a few taps.

Once she accepts, you pay securely inside the app — your money is held safely and only released after the club entry is successfully confirmed. No risk, no stress.

How it works

  1. 1 Browse Clubs — Discover bars, lounges, and clubs near you based on your current location.
  2. 2 Select a Companion — View verified profiles and choose someone you're comfortable with.
  3. 3 Send a Request — Reach out with a club entry request in seconds.
  4. 4 Pay the Fee — Complete the in-app payment once she accepts. Funds are secured until entry is confirmed.
  5. 5 Meet & Confirm — Arrive at the venue, meet at the entrance, and share the meetup code to complete the entry together.
For Her

For Her

STAG puts you in full control. You decide who you respond to, when you're available, and which requests you accept — no pressure, no obligations.

Enable your location, receive requests from nearby users, review every detail before responding, and earn directly once a meetup is confirmed. Your exact location is never shared — only your approximate area is visible to others.

How it works

  1. 1 Complete Your Profile — Add a photo and your details so users nearby can discover you.
  2. 2 Enable Location — Share your approximate area to appear in nearby searches.
  3. 3 Receive Requests — Get notified when someone wants to enter a club with you.
  4. 4 Accept or Decline — You're always in control. Accept only what you're comfortable with.
  5. 5 Meet at the Club — Arrive at the venue at the agreed time.
  6. 6 Confirm & Get Paid — Share the meetup code to confirm entry. Your earnings are transferred directly to your account.

Built on Trust

Every user on STAG is verified. Payments are held in escrow and only released once both parties confirm the meetup — protecting everyone involved. Your safety and comfort are not an afterthought; they are built into every step of the process.
